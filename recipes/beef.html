Beef Buger

Description

A recipe to make a beef burger

Ingredients

  • 1 pounds lean ground beef
  • onion, finely chopped
  • 1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill on high heat.
  2. Mix together ground beef, onion, cheese, onion soup mix, egg, garlic, garlic powder
  3. Cook patties on the preheated grill until well-done, about 5 minutes per side.
